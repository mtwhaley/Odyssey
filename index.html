Welcome to Odyssey Counseling and Consulting!

Your Journey to Wellness Starts Here.

Life is a long and eventful journey, one filled full of highs, lows and challenges. At Odyssey Counseling and Consulting we are a team of experienced and compassionate therapists who are dedicated to helping you navigate life's challenges. Our practice is founded on the belief that mental is not a one-time event but instead a journey that requires compassionate professionals willing to help you navigate the terrains of life when you become lost. Whether it is grief, anxiety, depression, trauma, or a range of other life stressors allow us to be a part of your story. Everyone deserves to live a fulfilling and meaningful life, and we are here to support you on your journey.

At Odyssey Counseling and Consulting, we believe that therapy is a collaborative process, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where you can feel comfortable sharing your thoughts and feelings. We are committed to providing you with the highest quality care, and we look forward to working with you!

We offer a range of services to meet your unique needs, including individual therapy, couples counseling, and family therapy. Our therapists are trained in a variety of evidence-based approaches, and we work collaboratively with you to identify strengths and develop a treatment plan that is tailored to your specific needs.
