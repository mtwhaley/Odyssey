Terry Terrell LMSW

Terry Terrell, LMSW is a Licensed Master Social Worker that provides therapy for pre-teens (10+), teens, adults and seniors dealing with depression, anger, anxiety, trauma and relationship conflicts. Terry has more than a decade of experience working with families and children. With vast experience in the field Terry has won several awards working with families and youth in Wichita Kansas and its surrounding areas. He has worked with local courts and gained vital knowledge, experience and skills in the preservation of families, family centered treatment and reintegration of children back into homes. Upon graduating with his Masters from Wichita State University, Terry worked in the permanency Clinic as a Trauma informed Reintegration therapist for two years. Terry's goal is to honor and empower his clients through guidance and support, so that they walk away empowered to navigate the complexity of life with the right tools to build their future.
